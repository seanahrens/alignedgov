Help us build the most comprehensive map of the AI & Democracy ecosystem.

What makes a good submission?

We're looking for resources relevant to AI governance, democracy tech, or the intersection of AI and democratic institutions. All types are welcome:

  • Research papers and reports
  • Organizations and nonprofits working in this space
  • Tools and platforms for democratic participation
  • People doing important work at this intersection
  • Original sources preferred over aggregators

What to expect

After you submit, our editorial team will review your resource. Approved links typically appear on the site within one week. We review every submission and curate for quality and relevance.
